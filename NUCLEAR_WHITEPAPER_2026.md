## 5 Insights That Matter

### 1. Big Tech Is the New Nuclear Customer

Forget utilities. The real demand signal is coming from hyperscalers:

| Microsoft | Helion fusion PPA (2028) | First-ever commercial fusion contract |

**The insight:** Nuclear's business model is shifting from regulated utility sales to corporate PPAs. This changes everything—procurement, financing, siting.

---

### 2. The Bottleneck Isn't Reactors—It's Fuel

Everyone's talking about SMRs. Nobody's talking about HALEU.

**The math:**
- Advanced reactors need High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium (HALEU)
- U.S. has **zero** commercial HALEU production
- Russia controlled 40%+ of global enrichment (now sanctioned)
- Centrus is the only U.S. company producing HALEU—at demo scale

**The insight:** The SMR gold rush will hit a wall in 2027-2028 when HALEU demand exceeds supply. Companies solving the fuel problem (Centrus, Urenco expansion) are the real bottleneck-breakers.

---

### 3. NRC Reform = Faster Deployment (Maybe)

The May 2025 EO orders NRC to:
- Cut licensing timelines
- Reduce "unnecessary regulatory burden"
- Prioritize advanced reactor approvals

**The reality check:**
- NRC reform takes years to implement
- Safety culture can't change overnight
- Industry still needs to prove designs work

**The insight:** Don't bet on NRC speed. Bet on companies that design for regulatory simplicity (fewer novel components = faster approval). NuScale's delays prove that "first" doesn't mean "fast."

---

### 4. Fusion Is Real Now (Sort Of)

2025-2026 changed fusion from "30 years away" to "possibly this decade":

| Company | Milestone | Timeline |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Helion | Microsoft PPA signed | 2028 target |
| Commonwealth Fusion | SPARC demo construction | 2025-2026 |
| TAE Technologies | Google partnership, $1.2B raised | 2030s commercial |

**The insight:** Fusion isn't competing with fission—it's complementary. Fission solves 2025-2035. Fusion (if it works) solves 2035+. Smart money is in both.

### 5. The Real Opportunity: Nuclear + AI Operations

Here's what nobody's talking about:

**Nuclear plants are OT-intensive, AI-ready environments.**

Every reactor needs:
- Instrumentation & Control (I&C) systems
- Safety-Instrumented Systems (SIS)
- Predictive maintenance
- Digital twins for training/simulation
- Cybersecurity (NRC 10 CFR 73.54)

**The insight:** The nuclear buildout isn't just construction—it's a massive OT modernization. AI for QA documentation, predictive maintenance, and licensing acceleration is the real value unlock. Physical construction is solved; compliance throughput is the bottleneck.

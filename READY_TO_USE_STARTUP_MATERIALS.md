## 4. LINKEDIN POST TEMPLATES

### Post 1: Industry Insights
```
Seeing reports of 24+ month lead times on large power transformers. Wild times for anyone building out data centers or industrial capacity. Anyone found creative solutions?
```

### Post 2: Educational
```
Quick primer: What makes a used transformer valuable vs. scrap?

• Testing history (Doble tests, oil analysis)
• Age and condition
• Voltage class and MVA rating
• Cooling type (ONAN, ONAF, OFAF)
• Tap changer functionality

Most buyers need refurbished/tested units, not "as-is" scrap.
```

### Post 3: Question/Engagement
```
Data center developers: What's your biggest equipment bottleneck right now? Transformers? Switchgear? Cooling? Curious what's gating timelines.
```

### Post 4: Market Intelligence
```
Noticing a pattern: Utilities upgrading to smart grid tech are decommissioning perfectly good transformers. These are gold for data centers/fabs that need power fast. If you're a utility asset manager, let's talk.
```

### Post 5: SDVOSB Angle
```
The AI infrastructure buildout is a national priority. As a certified SDVOSB, I'm helping federal agencies source critical equipment (transformers, switchgear, generators) faster than OEM lead times. If you're a contracting officer, let's connect.
```
